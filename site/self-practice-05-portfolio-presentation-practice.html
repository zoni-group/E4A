NoteWhy Self-Practice 5 Comes Before Class 5

This is on purpose. You will use the work from Self-Practice 5 to be ready for Class 5. In Class 5 you will combine your toolkit and present it. This self-practice helps you arrive at Class 5 with a draft and a complete portfolio folder.

11.1 Start Here

You are almost at Class 5 — the final class. Today is the time to bring all your work together. Open your toolkit folder and look at how far you have come. Each file is something you wrote and improved with your own English.

Today you will check that your portfolio is complete, choose your final project task, and write a first draft of your English presentation. You will leave this practice ready for Class 5.

11.2 What You Will Practice

  1. Checking your toolkit folder against the Before Class 5 checklist.
  2. Choosing one real, safe task for your final project.
  3. Writing the first draft of a short English presentation.
  4. Reading your draft aloud and finding one word to practice.
