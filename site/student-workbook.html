TipUse This Page When…

Use this page when you need to copy a worksheet template, set up a portfolio file, or check the final submission list. Every class and self-practice has a matching worksheet block below.

NoteTrack Your Progress

This workbook does not repeat the full progress table. Track your work in two places:

  • The My Progress table in the Orientation chapter — check off each class, self-practice, and reference file as you finish it.
  • The Final Submission Checklist at the bottom of this workbook — use it at the end to confirm every required file is in your English AI Decision Toolkit folder.

If a row is missing or unclear, ask your teacher.

TipAI Check Boxes (Optional)

Some Optional Colab Labs use Colab AI when it is available. Use this short checklist every time you see an AI answer in a lab. You can also copy these boxes next to any class artifact where AI helped you.

You do not need successful AI generation to finish your portfolio. If Colab AI is unavailable, finish the regular lab step and tick the boxes that still apply.

This workbook collects the major student worksheets for the course. Your teacher may give you this as a website, PDF, Word file, or printed packet.

15.1 Student Course Overview

In this course, you will practice English while you learn safe AI skills. You will write prompts, compare options, check sources, describe visuals, write a speaking script, and present a final toolkit.

Your final portfolio is called:

English AI Decision Toolkit

15.2 Portfolio Setup Instructions

  1. Create a folder named English AI Decision Toolkit.
  2. Save every class and self-practice file in this folder.
  3. Use clear file names.
  4. Save important work outside the AI tool.
  5. Do not save private documents in the folder.

15.18 Personal AI Safety Checklist

Before I use AI, I ask:

  1. Is this task safe for AI?
  2. Am I sharing private information?
  3. Do I need a source?
  4. Could this affect money, health, legal status, school, or work?
  5. What would happen if the AI is wrong?
  6. How will I verify the answer?
  7. What final decision is still mine?
  8. Can I explain my answer in English?
