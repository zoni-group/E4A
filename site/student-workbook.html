TipAI Check Boxes

Use this short checklist every time AI helps you.

You do not need successful AI generation to finish your portfolio.

This workbook collects the major student worksheets for English for AI: 10 Lessons + Homework. Your teacher may give you this as a website, PDF, Word file, or printed packet.

15.1 Student Course Overview

In this course, you will practice English while you learn safe AI skills. You will write prompts, compare options, check sources, describe visuals, write a speaking script, and present a final toolkit.

Your final portfolio is called:

English AI Decision Toolkit

15.2 Workbook Use Instructions

  1. If you use the website, type directly in the worksheet boxes.
  2. If you use paper, write directly on the printed worksheet pages.
  3. Keep your workbook outside any AI tool.
  4. Follow your teacher’s collection instructions.
  5. Do not put private documents or private details in your workbook.

Your answers are saved only in this browser on this device. They are not sent to ZONI or your teacher from this page. If you use a shared computer, ask your teacher how to collect your work, then clear your saved answers.
